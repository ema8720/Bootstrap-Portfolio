About Me

My name is John Correa and I am a soon to be full-stack Web Developer, currently living in Los Angeles, CA. I have a Bachelor of Communication in Commercial Advertising and Design from University of Puerto Rico, and my current primary focus and inspiration for my studies is Web Development. In my free time, I study human computer interface and the psychology of human computer interaction.

I am both driven and self-motivated, and I am constantly experimenting with new technologies and techniques. I am very passionate about Web Development, and strive to better myself as a developer. I Work as a Digital Content Marketing Manager for the Automotive Industry. My goal is to create a platform that will make a more efficient communication between the dealerships and the customers.
